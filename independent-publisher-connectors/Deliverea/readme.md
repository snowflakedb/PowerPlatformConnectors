\# Deliverea Connector



The Deliverea connector allows you to easily integrate Deliverea with Microsoft Power Automate.  

Using this connector, you can automate delivery-related processes and connect Deliverea with other services and workflows in your organization.



\## Publisher



IFR



\## Prerequisites



To use this connector, you must:



\- Have an active Deliverea account.

\- Have access to Microsoft Power Automate.

\- Have the necessary permissions in Deliverea to access the API.



\## Supported Operations



The Deliverea connector enables you to interact with Deliverea through the following types of actions and triggers (depending on your configuration):



\- Create a shipment.

\- Cancel a shipment.

\- Get a shipment label.

\- Get distribution centers.

\- Get allowed carriers in a distribution center.



> Note: The available operations depend on the API permissions associated with your Deliverea account.



\## Authentication



This connector uses Deliverea account credentials for authentication.



\### How to get credentials



To obtain your credentials:



1\. Register or log in at \[https://www.deliverea.com](https://www.deliverea.com).

2\. Ensure your account has API access enabled.

3\. Use the credentials provided by Deliverea (such as API key, username/password, or token, depending on your setup).



If you do not have API credentials, please contact Deliverea support for assistance.



\## Known Limitations and Considerations



\- API rate limits may apply depending on your Deliverea plan.

\- Some operations may not be available for all account types.

\- Data returned by the connector is subject to Deliverea API availability and response times.



\## Common Errors and Troubleshooting



\- \*\*Authentication failed\*\*: Verify that your credentials are correct and active.

\- \*\*Unauthorized access\*\*: Ensure your account has the required permissions.

\- \*\*Timeouts or connection errors\*\*: Check Deliverea service status and your network connectivity.



\## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



\*\*Q: Do I need a paid Deliverea account to use this connector?\*\*  

A: API access may depend on your Deliverea subscription plan. Please check with Deliverea support.



\*\*Q: Can I use this connector in both cloud and automated flows?\*\*  

A: Yes, the connector can be used in cloud flows and automated workflows, subject to Power Automate limitations.



\*\*Q: Who should I contact for support?\*\*  

A: For connector-related issues, contact Deliverea support. For Power Automate issues, refer to Microsoft documentation.
